Biology

Hello! I’m Rylee Rol, a third-year Biology major at the University of California, Riverside, with a focus on medicine and dermatology. Driven by a passion for healthcare, I am actively pursuing opportunities that combine scientific knowledge with compassionate patient care. My academic journey has provided me with hands-on experience in Biology, Chemistry, and Organic Chemistry labs, where I’ve honed technical skills such as TLC, gel electrophoresis, micro pipetting, microscopy, centrifugation, and cell culture—skills I am eager to expand and apply as I progress toward a career in healthcare.

In my current role as a Behavior Specialist at the Center for Social Dynamics (CSD), I work one-on-one with clients to support and empower them in developing social, emotional, and behavioral skills. This role has deepened my understanding of individualized care and strengthened my commitment to making a difference in others' lives, especially in areas of behavior and well-being. Additionally, I work as an Intramural Referee at the SRC, where I bring my leadership and communication skills to foster a fair, enjoyable environment for students participating in sports activities. This position has taught me the importance of teamwork, resilience, and adaptability—qualities that are invaluable in both personal and professional settings. Beyond my professional work, I dedicate considerable time to volunteering, where I support local health initiatives and engage directly with my community. My extensive volunteer experience, including roles with the HUGS program and Shriners Hospital, has been both rewarding and formative, allowing me to connect with patients of all ages and backgrounds.

Currently, I am seeking further clinical experience to deepen my understanding of medicine, with a specific interest in dermatology. My goal is to help bridge the gap between science and compassionate care, working to make a positive impact on patients’ lives by supporting their skin health and overall well-being. Thank you for visiting my page—I look forward to connecting with professionals who share a commitment to healthcare, community health, and patient advocacy!
